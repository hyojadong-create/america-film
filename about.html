About America Film

A wholesale film supplier built for professionals

We supply tint shops, detailers, distributors, and dealerships with premium window film and paint protection — and the consistency and support that keep their businesses running.

America Film is a wholesale supplier of automotive window tint and paint protection film. We focus on one thing: giving installers and resellers film that performs the same way on every roll, ships on time, and is priced to keep your business profitable.

From a single tint shop to a high-volume distributor, our customers count on America Film for steady stock, fast nationwide shipping, and flexible order quantities — whether you need five rolls or fifty.

Premium materials inside

Quality starts with what the film is made of. Our rolls are engineered from globally recognized components: optically clear, dimensionally stable PET base film from Toray and Nan Ya; strong, low-haze adhesive from Bostik and Ashland; and a scratch-resistant hard coat from Toray and Cashew. The result is film that shrinks cleanly, lays flat, and keeps its clarity and color year after year.

Non-metallic technology

Our nano-ceramic and carbon films are non-metallic, so they won't interfere with GPS, Bluetooth, or cellular signal. Every batch is tested for even coating, consistent shade, and lasting heat and UV rejection.

Built for the install bay

Installers tell us the same thing: our film is easy to work with. Smooth slip, predictable shrink, plotter-friendly liners, and a durable adhesive that won't haze — the details that save time and cut down on rework.

Who we serve

Tint shops, auto detailers, car dealerships, fleet managers, and retail distributors across the country rely on America Film for window tint and PPF that delivers comfort, privacy, and protection their customers can count on.
